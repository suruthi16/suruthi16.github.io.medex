Drug Description

Eucrisa

The generic name of eucrisa is Crisaborole

Used for:

Mild to moderate atopic dermatitis


Age:

Above 3 months


Consumption amount:

20 mg per day, and should continue to maximum of 8 weeks.

Side effects:

The common side effects include burning or stinging in skin. Rarely there may be some allergic reaction.

Ingredients used:

  1. white petrolatum
  2. propylene glycol
  3. mono and di glycerides
  4. paraffin
  5. butylated hydroxy toluene
  6. calcium dioxide
